Who we are

We, team of Lacmus project, are developing and providing assistance in deployment of modern Deep Learning solutions intended for supporting search-and-rescue operations aimed at people lost in uninhabited terrain (fields, forests, etc.)

In collaboration with search and rescue volunteer groups we're developing software to help teams finding people on UAV (unmanned autonomous vehicles, or simply drones) photos. As machine learning professionals we're trying to do the search process as automated and fast as possible.

find out more (in Russian)

What are we doing

Gathering data

In coordination with S&R teams we’re gathering photos, labeling them, and collecting into datasets.

Training neural networks

We're using state-of the art deep-learning models and algorithms to achieve the best results possible.

Optimizing models

Solutions delivered to field operation are further optimized to achieve the best speed.
